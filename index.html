About

This site will serve as the guideline for the project titled "Cubing Teacher". The project is designed to be a one stop shop for all your cubing needs. Primarily a way to teach anybody interested on how they can go about solving any cube on the market. For those unaware cubes come in all different shapes and sizes. In most cases the more complex the cube shape, the harder it becomes to solve. To provide our users with the ability to solve any of these cubes will allow for anyone to jump into this hobby!

Within the Content tab we will have laid out the general design layout for our site. This will include:

  • Colors and patterns
  • Typography
  • Buttons
  • Layouts

Along with the above, we will also provide more info in our FAQ section.

We thank you for taking the time of viewing the page and hope you enjoy the behind the scenes of Cubing Teacher!
